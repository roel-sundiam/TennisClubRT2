Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the new system

Will my existing account and data be transferred?

+

Yes! All your membership information, booking history, and account details have been safely transferred to the new system. You can use your existing login credentials.

Can I use this on my phone or tablet?

+

Absolutely! The new system is designed mobile-first, meaning it works perfectly on all devices - phones, tablets, and computers. You can even install it as an app on your phone.

What are coins and how do they work?

+

Coins are our new reward system. You earn coins for activities like making bookings and participating in club events. You can use coins for special features and discounts.

Is the new system secure?

+

Yes! We've implemented the latest security measures including encrypted payments, secure user authentication, and regular security updates to keep your data safe.

What if I need help using the new system?

+

Our support team is here to help! Contact the club administration for assistance, or use the built-in help features in the new app.
